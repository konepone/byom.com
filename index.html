My Approach

How clarity emerges

My work looks at you, your relationships, and your context at the same time. Change starts from who you are and how you show up, then moves into what you do and what you create.

I combine methods from systemic coaching, integral frameworks, and relational leadership with a very present, human way of working. Sessions are structured but not scripted.

The space is clear, honest, and kind. I name what I see, ask precise questions, and stay with you when it gets uncomfortable, without pushing past your boundaries.

You are active in the process. Over time this builds awareness, choice, and new ways of relating. Insight is important, but the goal is lived change.

What guides this work

  • CourageNaming what is real, even when it is uncomfortable.
  • ClaritySimplifying the complex so people can act.
  • ConnectionWorking with people, not roles.
  • LightnessDepth without heaviness.
  • ResponsibilityShowing up consistently and honestly.
About

Karin Gutenbrunner Byom

I grew up on a pioneering organic farm in Upper Austria, shaped by responsibility, community, and the courage to try new paths. For many years I initiated and supported civic and community projects, and later worked in project management, change management, and agile organisational development across different sectors.

Since 2019 I have been working independently as a coach and facilitator, grounded in solid coach training and ongoing education in counselling and supervision. I am trained in systemic and integral coaching and in relational leadership.

A severe Long Covid episode in 2022 deepened my practice. Months of enforced, almost complete sensory reduction showed me what it means when orientation, energy, and what used to be self-evident fall away, and what people need in order to regain their agency step by step.

I work with people and organisations at turning points and in phases of uncertainty, pressure, or new beginnings. My way of working is direct and warm, professionally clear and human in tone.

Tilnærming

Hvordan klarhet oppstår

Arbeidet mitt ser på deg, relasjonene dine og konteksten din samtidig. Endring starter med hvem du er og hvordan du viser deg, og beveger seg deretter til hva du gjør og skaper.

Jeg kombinerer metoder fra systemisk coaching, integrale rammeverk og relasjonell ledelse med en svært nærværende, menneskelig arbeidsmåte. Samtalene er strukturerte, men ikke skriptede.

Rommet er klart, ærlig og vennlig. Jeg navngir det jeg ser, stiller presise spørsmål og blir med deg når det blir ubehagelig, uten å presse forbi grensene dine.

Du er aktiv i prosessen. Over tid bygger dette bevissthet, valgfrihet og nye måter å forholde seg på. Innsikt er viktig, men målet er levd endring.

  • MotNavngi det som er virkelig, selv når det er ubehagelig.
  • KlarhetForenkle det komplekse slik at folk kan handle.
  • ForbindelseJobbe med mennesker, ikke roller.
  • LetthetDybde uten tyngde.
